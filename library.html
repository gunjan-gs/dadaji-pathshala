Library Facilities

Reading Rooms

Spacious and well-lit reading rooms with comfortable seating arrangements designed for focused study sessions. Climate-controlled environment ensures optimal learning conditions throughout the year.

High-Speed Wi-Fi

24/7 high-speed internet connectivity throughout the library for accessing online resources, digital libraries, and research databases. Seamless connectivity for all your academic needs.

Extensive Collection

Over 50,000 books covering various subjects including technical, reference, fiction, and general literature. Regular additions of latest publications and bestsellers keep our collection current.

Digital Resources

Access to premium e-journals, e-books, and online databases for comprehensive research. Subscriptions to major academic publishers and digital libraries enhance your learning experience.

Group Study Spaces

Dedicated areas for collaborative learning and group discussions. Equipped with whiteboards and presentation tools, these spaces foster teamwork and knowledge sharing.

Extended Hours

Open Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM with extended hours during examination periods. Flexible timings accommodate diverse study schedules and preferences.
